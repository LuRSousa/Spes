SEEDS OF HOPE, CRUCIAL SOLUTIONS TO PRESERVE INDIGENOUS HABITATS

Learn More

The native habitats conservation is a complex and multifaceted issue.

The indigenous lands delimitation is an important step to protect this habitats and ensure the survival of the communities that live there.

In addition, its essencial to implement public politics to improve the quality of life of these population groups.

The use of digital solutions can also be an important way to preserve the native habitats. For example, The Agrotools offers the solutions to identify overlapping lands and protect indigenous areas.

Another important strategy is the creationg of protect natural areas, such as natinal parks and biological reserves.

These areas are important to preserve the biodiversity and can help protecting the native habitats.

Natural resources management is also essential to preserve the habitat

The United Nations emphasize that the indigenous people are essential actors in the environmental protection and that their traditional practices on the lands management can be a valuable source of knowledge to preserve the biological diversity.

Finally, is important to emphasize that the indigenous habitats conservation it's not just an abiental issue, but also an human rights issue. The protection of these habitats is necessary to ensure the survival of these communities who lives there and to promote the cultural diversity and the respect for the diversity.
